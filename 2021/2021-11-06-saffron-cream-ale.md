# 🟡 Saffron cream ale

> 📝 Cream ale with saffron.

**Label:** SCA

##  ⚖️ Ingredient (1 gallon)

### 🌾 Grains

* 650 g 2-row malt
* 200 g flaked corn

### 🌿 Hops

* 6 g Styrian Golding

### 🧫 Yeast

* Fermentis SafAle US-05

### 🍃 Extra

* 1 g of saffron

## 📖 Recipe

### 🚰 Mash

* Mash 3 L at 73 °C for 1 hour (it should stay at 67 °C)
* Drain into mash tun
* Add 3 L at 76 °C for 15 minutes into the mash
* Drain into mash tun

### ⏱  Boil

* Boil for 1 hour
* At 0 minutes, add 6 g Styrian Golding

### ⚗️ Fermentation

* Cool down to 20 °C
* Syphon into carboy
* Add yeast and gently mix
